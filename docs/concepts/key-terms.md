# Key terms

A quick orientation to the words you'll see across AlignOne. The full list, with the exact
in-app wording, is in the [Glossary](../reference/glossary.md).

- **Family / care circle** — the foster/adoptive family being served, together with the
  advocate and volunteers supporting them.
- **Need** — a specific request for help from a family (a meal, a ride, supplies).
- **Claim** — signing up to handle a need.
- **Schedule** — the family's calendar of events, including recurring commitments.
- **Thread** — a private message conversation; a **message** is one entry in it.
- **Post** — an update shared with the care circle; people can **comment** on it.
- **Training module** — a unit of training that prepares people to serve.
- **Advocate**, **Volunteer**, **Coordinator** — roles. See
  [Roles & who sees what](roles-and-visibility.md).
