# Sign up for a slot

**Who this is for:** Volunteers (advocates and program staff can do this too).
**When to use it:** When the calendar has a slot you can cover.
**Before you start:** You can [view the calendar](view-calendar.md) for your family.

!!! info "Screenshots coming"
    These steps are described in words for now; annotated screenshots land once the screens
    are final.

## Steps

1. Open **Schedule** and find a slot that still needs someone.
2. Tap the slot to open it and read the details — **what's needed**, **when**, and
   **where**.
3. Make sure you can commit to the day and time.
4. Choose **Sign up**.  <!-- @frontend: confirm the button label + confirm-dialog copy -->
5. Confirm. The slot now shows **your name** as the person covering it.

## What you'll see

The slot shows you as signed up, it appears in your own list of commitments, and the rest
of the team can see it's covered.

!!! tip "If your plans change"
    Let the team know as early as you can so someone else can take the slot.
