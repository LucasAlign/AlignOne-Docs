# Withdraw a claim

**Who this is for:** Volunteers (advocates and program staff can do this too).
**When to use it:** When you've claimed a need but can no longer do it, so someone else
can step in.
**Before you start:** You've [claimed a need](claim-a-need.md) that you now need to release.
You can withdraw any time **before the need is marked complete**.

!!! info "Screenshots coming"
    These steps are described in words for now; annotated screenshots land once the screens
    are final.

## Steps

1. Open **Needs** and find the need you claimed — it shows **your name** as the person
   handling it.  <!-- @frontend: confirm where a volunteer's own claimed needs appear -->
2. Open the need and choose **Withdraw**.  <!-- @frontend: confirm the button label + confirm-dialog copy -->
3. Confirm. Your claim is removed and the need returns to the **open** list.

## What you'll see

The need no longer shows your name and is open for someone else to claim. It also drops
off your own list of commitments.

!!! tip "Withdraw early"
    The sooner you let go of a need you can't do, the more time the team has to find
    someone else. A quick note in [the message thread](../messaging/start-thread.md) helps too.
