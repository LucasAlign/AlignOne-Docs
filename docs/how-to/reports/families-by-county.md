# Families Served by County

**Who this is for:** Program staff (Admins and Coordinators).
**What it is:** A chart on the **Reports & Impact** page.

## What it shows

This bar chart breaks your families down into groups so you can compare them side by side. By default it groups families by county, with one bar per county showing how many families fall into each.

## Options you can change

You reach these via the gear (⚙) on the panel while in Customize mode.

- **Group by** — choose how the bars are split: by **County** (the default), by **Church**, or by **Status**.
- **Show % of total** — adds the percentage of all families to each bar, so you can see each group's share at a glance.

## Which filters affect it

**County** and **Church** apply and narrow which families are included in the breakdown. **Date range does not apply** — this chart reflects each family's current status, and the app doesn't keep a history of when a status changed, so there's no past window to count.

## Tips & notes

!!! note "Grouping and filtering work together"
    If you filter to a single county, grouping **by County** will show just that one bar. To compare across counties, leave the County filter on *All Counties*.
