# Families by Church

**Who this is for:** Program staff (Admins and Coordinators).
**What it is:** A chart on the **Reports & Impact** page.

## What it shows

This bar chart shows how many families each serving church supports. Every church that has families assigned to it gets its own bar, so you can see at a glance which churches carry the most care communities and how the load is spread.

## Options you can change

While in Customize mode, select the gear (⚙) on the panel to adjust:

- **Show top** — limit the chart to the busiest churches (top 5, 8, 10, or 15) or show all churches. Useful when you have many churches and want to focus on the leaders.

## Which filters affect it

**County** and **Church** apply, so you can focus on a single county or a single church. **Date range does not apply** — this chart reflects each family's current serving church, and the app doesn't keep a history of when a family was assigned, so there's no past window to count.

## Tips & notes

!!! note "Families without a church aren't shown"
    Families that don't have a serving church assigned don't appear on this chart. If your totals look low, check whether some families are still missing a church.
