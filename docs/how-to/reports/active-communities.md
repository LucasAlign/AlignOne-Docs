# Active Care Communities

**Who this is for:** Program staff (Admins and Coordinators).
**What it is:** A counter tile on the **Reports & Impact** page.

## What it shows

This tile counts the families whose status means they're actively being served. By default it counts families that are **Active** or **Launched**, giving you a quick read on how many care communities are up and running.

## Options you can change

While in Customize mode, select the **gear (⚙)** on the panel to change what gets counted:

- **Active statuses** — use the checkboxes to choose exactly which statuses count as "active." Check or uncheck statuses so the tile matches how your program defines an active community.

## Which filters affect it

- **County** and **Church** apply — they narrow the tile to the families in that county or served by that church.
- **Date range does not apply.** The tile reflects each family's current status, and the app doesn't keep a history of when a status changed, so there's no past window to count.

## Tips & notes

!!! note "It's a snapshot"
    The number shows how many communities are active right now, based on their current statuses — not how many were active during a chosen period.
