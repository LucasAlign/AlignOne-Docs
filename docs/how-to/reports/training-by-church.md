# Training Completion by Church

**Who this is for:** Program staff (Admins and Coordinators).
**What it is:** A chart on the **Reports & Impact** page.

## What it shows

This stacked bar chart compares trained volunteers against those still in progress at each church. Every church gets a bar split into two parts, so you can see how far along each church's volunteers are with their training.

## Options you can change

While in Customize mode, select the gear (⚙) on the panel to adjust:

- **Display** — show the numbers as a headcount (how many volunteers) or as a completion percentage (what share at each church is trained).
- **Show top** — limit the chart to a set number of churches so you can focus on the ones with the most volunteers.

## Which filters affect it

**County** and **Church** apply, so you can focus on a single county or a single church. **Date range does not apply** — volunteer records don't carry the dates needed to narrow by time.

## Tips & notes

!!! note "What \"trained\" means"
    Here, a volunteer counts as trained once they've completed onboarding. Volunteers still working through onboarding show up in the in-progress part of the bar.
