# Care Community Status

**Who this is for:** Program staff (Admins and Coordinators).
**What it is:** A chart on the **Reports & Impact** page.

## What it shows

This donut chart shows the mix of family statuses across your care communities. Each slice represents a group of families, so you can see at a glance how your families are distributed — for example, how many are active versus paused.

## Options you can change

You reach these via the gear (⚙) on the panel while in Customize mode.

- **By status** — shows one slice per status, for a detailed breakdown.
- **Active vs. inactive** — combines statuses into just two slices, for a simpler overview.
- **Show % in the legend** — adds each slice's percentage to the legend.

## Which filters affect it

**County** and **Church** apply and narrow which families are included. **Date range does not apply** — this chart reflects each family's current status, and the app doesn't keep a history of when a status changed, so there's no past window to count.

## Tips & notes

!!! tip "Switch views for different audiences"
    Use **Active vs. inactive** for a quick, high-level snapshot, and **By status** when you need to see exactly where families sit.
