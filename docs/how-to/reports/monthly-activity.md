# Monthly Support Activity Totals

**Who this is for:** Program staff (Admins and Coordinators).
**What it is:** A chart on the **Reports & Impact** page.

## What it shows

This bar chart shows the support delivered each month, broken down by type of need. Each need type (such as Meals or Transportation) gets its own colored series, so you can see both the overall monthly volume and which kinds of support drove it.

## Options you can change

While in Customize mode, select the gear (⚙) on the panel to adjust:

- **Bar layout** — show the need types **Stacked** in a single bar per month, or **Grouped** side by side so you can compare types directly.
- **Include open / unfilled needs** — by default the chart counts support that was delivered; turn this on to also include needs that are still open or unfilled.

## Which filters affect it

All three filters apply. **Date range** sets which months are shown, and **County** and **Church** narrow the activity to a single county or church.

## Tips & notes

!!! tip "Choose the layout that answers your question"
    Use **Stacked** to read total monthly volume at a glance, and **Grouped** when you want to compare how individual need types changed month to month.
