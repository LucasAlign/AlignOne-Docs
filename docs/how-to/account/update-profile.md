# Update your profile

**Who this is for:** Everyone with an AlignOne account.
**When to use it:** After you first sign in, or any time your details change.
**Before you start:** You've [accepted your invite and signed in](accept-invite.md).

!!! info "Screenshots coming"
    These steps are described in words for now; annotated screenshots land once the screens
    are final.

## Steps

1. Open your **profile**.  <!-- @frontend: confirm where profile lives (avatar menu? settings?) and the exact label -->
2. Review and update your details — your **name**, **contact information**, and, if you're
   a volunteer, **how you'd like to help** and your **availability**.
   <!-- @frontend: confirm which fields are editable for a volunteer vs. staff -->
3. **Save** your changes.  <!-- @frontend: confirm the save button label + any confirmation -->

## What you'll see

Your updated details appear on your profile and to the rest of your care circle, so the
team can reach you and knows the kind of help you offer.

!!! tip "Keep availability current"
    If you'll be away or your schedule changes, update your availability so the team knows
    when to count on you.
