# FAQ

Quick answers to common questions. For step-by-step help, follow the linked guide. If
something isn't here, see [Troubleshooting](troubleshooting.md).

## Accounts & access

**How do I join AlignOne?**
The program emails you an invite. Open it, set a password, and sign in.
→ [Accept your invite & sign in](../how-to/account/accept-invite.md)

**My invite link doesn't work.**
Invite links expire (typically after 7 days). Ask the program to
[resend your invite](../how-to/admin/onboarding.md#resend-an-invite).

**Why can I only see one family?**
If you're a **volunteer**, you're meant to see just the family you're assigned to. That's
how AlignOne keeps every family's information private.
→ [Roles & who sees what](../concepts/roles-and-visibility.md)

**Can I support more than one family?**
A volunteer is assigned to one family at a time. If you need a different or additional
assignment, ask program staff.

## Needs

**What's a "need"?**
A specific task a family asked for help with — a meal, a ride, supplies, prayer.
→ [What is WrapAround?](../concepts/what-is-wraparound.md)

**I claimed a need but can't do it anymore.**
[Withdraw your claim](../how-to/needs/withdraw-a-claim.md) as early as you can so someone
else can step in. You can withdraw any time before the need is marked complete.

**What happens after I finish a need?**
[Mark it complete](../how-to/needs/mark-complete.md) so the family and team know it's done.

## Schedule

**What's a recurring event?**
An event that repeats on a rule, like a weekly ride. It appears on every date it applies to.
→ [Understand recurring events](../how-to/schedules/recurring-events.md)

## Messages & notifications

**Does a read receipt mean my request is handled?**
No — it means the message was *seen*, not that anyone took the task. Wait for a clear reply
or a claim/sign-up. → [Replies & read receipts](../how-to/messaging/reply-read-receipts.md)

**How do I stay on top of new activity?**
Check your [notifications](../how-to/messaging/notifications.md), and turn on alerts if
they're available.

## Training

**Why can't I serve yet, even though I finished training?**
Completing training marks you **trained but not active**. A staff member activates you
before you start serving. → [Find your modules & track progress](../how-to/training/modules-and-progress.md)

## For program staff

**A family registered themselves — why aren't they active?**
Self-registered families start at **Care Requested / Needs Vetting** and need staff
approval first. → [Onboard a family & invite people](../how-to/admin/onboarding.md)

**How do I give a volunteer extra abilities?**
Mark them a **Lead Volunteer** for their family.
→ [Manage volunteers & advocates](../how-to/admin/volunteers-and-advocates.md)
