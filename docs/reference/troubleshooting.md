# Troubleshooting

Help for the most common snags. If your problem isn't here, contact your program staff or
advocate.

## I didn't get my invite email

1. Check your **spam / junk** folder and search for AlignOne.
   <!-- @frontend: confirm the sender name/address so readers can search for it -->
2. Confirm the program has your **correct email address** — invites go to one specific
   address, and that address is how AlignOne connects you to the right family and role.
3. Ask program staff to [resend the invite](../how-to/admin/onboarding.md#resend-an-invite).

## My invite link expired or won't open

Invite links are time-limited (typically **7 days**). If yours has expired, don't keep
retrying the old link — ask staff to **resend** a fresh invite.

## I can't sign in

1. Make sure you're using the **same email** your invite was sent to.
2. Use **Sign in** and your email and password. Passwords are at least 8 characters with an
   uppercase letter, a number, and a symbol — a "wrong password" error is often just a typo
   in a long one.
   <!-- @frontend: confirm the sign-in button label and the password-reset path -->
3. If you've forgotten your password, use the **reset password** option on the sign-in
   screen. <!-- @frontend: confirm where password reset lives -->
4. Still stuck? Your account may not be fully set up yet — contact program staff.

## I'm signed in but can't see my family

- As a **volunteer**, you only see the family you're **assigned** to. If you see nothing,
  you may not be assigned yet — ask staff to
  [assign you to a family](../how-to/admin/volunteers-and-advocates.md#assign-a-volunteer-to-a-family).
- Seeing only one family when you expected more is usually **correct** for a volunteer.
  → [Roles & who sees what](../concepts/roles-and-visibility.md)

## I finished training but still can't serve

Completing training marks you **trained but not active**. A staff member needs to
**activate** you before you can be placed with a family.
→ [Find your modules & track progress](../how-to/training/modules-and-progress.md)

## A claim or sign-up didn't seem to save

1. Check your connection and try again.
2. Reopen the need or slot — if your **name** shows on it, it saved.
3. If it still won't take, post in [the message thread](../how-to/messaging/start-thread.md)
   so the team isn't left wondering whether it's covered.

## I think I see something I shouldn't

Family information is private and scoped by role on the server. If you believe you can see a
family you're not responsible for, **report it to program staff right away** — and don't
share what you saw.

## Still need help?

Contact your **program staff** or **advocate**. For how-to questions, the
[FAQ](faq.md) and the [How-To Guides](../how-to/needs/browse-needs.md) cover most tasks.
